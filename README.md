# Restaurant Website

## Overview

This project is a modern restaurant website developed to strengthen the connection between the restaurant and its customers across different parts of the city.

As the restaurant's popularity grew, many customers wanted an easier way to explore the menu, view food offerings, learn about the restaurant, and stay informed about available services without having to visit in person. This website was created to provide an engaging online experience that allows customers to discover the restaurant's dishes and interact with the business from anywhere.

## Features

* Interactive and responsive user interface
* Digital food menu with images and descriptions
* Restaurant information and story
* Contact and location details
* Mobile-friendly design
* Food gallery showcasing popular dishes
* Easy navigation for customers
* Online customer engagement platform

## How It Works

The website allows customers to:

* Browse the restaurant's menu online
* View food images and specialties
* Learn about the restaurant and its services
* Access contact information and location details
* Explore offerings before visiting the restaurant

By providing an online presence, the restaurant can reach a larger audience and offer customers a convenient way to interact with the business.

## Purpose

The main goal of this project was to help a well-known restaurant expand its reach and improve customer engagement. The website serves as a digital showcase of the restaurant's food, atmosphere, and services, allowing customers from different areas of the city to explore what the restaurant offers before deciding to visit.

## Technologies Used

* Responsive Web Design
